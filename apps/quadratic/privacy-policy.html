Privacy Policy

Welcome to Quadratic! This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our app.

Data We Collect

Quadratic collects only anonymous usage details to help us improve the app. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • How often the app is used
  • General feature usage patterns
  • App performance metrics

Note: We do not collect any personal data such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or device-specific identifiers.

How We Use the Data

The anonymous data we collect is used solely to:

  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Understand how users interact with features
  • Identify and fix bugs

Data Sharing and Security

We value your privacy. Therefore:

  • We do not share any collected data with third parties.
  • All data is processed in a way that ensures anonymity and does not identify any individual user.
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect the data collected.

Your Rights

Since no personal data is collected, Quadratic does not maintain data records that can be traced back to any individual. If you have concerns about app usage data, feel free to contact us using the information below.
