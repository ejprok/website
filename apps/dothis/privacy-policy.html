Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 4, 2024

Do This ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

What Data We Collect

We collect non-personally identifiable information that helps us understand how our users interact with our app and how we can improve their experience. This includes:

  • Interaction Data: We collect information about which screens you visit within the app, but we do not track the tasks you enter.
  • Device Information: We collect information about the device you use, such as the device model, operating system version, and app version.

How We Use This Data

We use this data solely to:

  • Improve the performance and functionality of our app.
  • Identify and fix bugs or issues.
  • Understand how users engage with the app to make better-informed decisions about future updates.

No personally identifiable information (PPI) is collected, and the data we gather cannot be used to identify you. We do not share, sell, or use this data for advertising purposes.

Data Security

We are committed to protecting the data we collect. Since no PPI is collected, the data is anonymized and poses no risk to your privacy.

User Rights

You have the right to opt-out of analytics tracking if you wish. Please contact us for further details.

Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of any changes to this privacy policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
